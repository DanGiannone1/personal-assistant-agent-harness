# Agentic Design

After adoption, read `docs/agentic-design.md` in full before changing agent
definitions, skills, orchestration roles, or handoffs. It is the canonical
design guidance. If it is missing, unreadable, or conflicts with active
instructions, stop and report; do not recreate or infer it.
